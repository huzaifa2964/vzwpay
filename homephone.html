Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to keep my existing phone number?

No, you can keep your existing phone number. We offer number porting services to transfer your current number to our service at no extra cost.

What equipment do I need for home phone service?

You'll need a compatible phone and we provide the necessary equipment including the modem and any required adapters. Professional installation is included.

Can I use my existing phones?

Yes, most standard home phones are compatible with our service. Our technicians will test your existing equipment during installation.

Is there a contract required?

We offer both contract and no-contract options. Choose the plan that best fits your needs with flexible terms available.

What happens during a power outage?

Our service includes backup power systems to keep your phone working during power outages, ensuring you stay connected in emergencies.
