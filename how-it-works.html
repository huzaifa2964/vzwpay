How It Works

Getting started with Alpha Solutions Hub is simple. Follow these four easy steps to explore and order internet and TV services in your area.

1

Enter Your Address

Provide your service location so we can check which providers may offer service in your area. This helps us identify available options based on your specific location.

  • Enter your complete street address
  • Include apartment or unit number if applicable
  • Confirm your service location details
2

View Available Providers

We'll show you the service providers that may be available at your address, along with their plan options and typical pricing ranges.

  • See which providers serve your area
  • Review available plan options
  • Compare pricing ranges and features
3

Choose Your Plan

Select the service package that best fits your needs. You can choose from internet-only, TV-only, or bundled packages depending on availability.

  • Compare different service tiers
  • Review plan features and pricing
  • Select your preferred package
4

Schedule Installation

Once you've selected a plan, we'll help coordinate with the provider to schedule installation at a time that works for you.

  • Choose a convenient installation date
  • Receive confirmation details
  • Prepare for service activation

What to Expect

Service Availability

Not all providers serve all areas. We'll show you which services may be available at your specific location.

Pricing Confirmation

Final pricing is confirmed by the service provider during the ordering process and may vary based on promotions.

Installation Process

Most providers offer professional installation. Installation timing and any associated fees are determined by the provider.
